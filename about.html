ABOUT ME

I am Jeannine Uwase from the Bigogwe district in the Western province of Rwanda. I was grown up in an environment where kids treat themselves in terms of feeding themselves during the day, and parents provide for supper. This wasn't the wish of my parents but because of the situation, we have just stayed in that place which was very bad for everyone in my family. At the age of 6, we moved to a new place called Kabana where I got the chance to attend a primary school called Rega Catholique. I was hungry to study since I knew that all educated people live in the city so I also wanted to study well so that I can be able to live in a place where I would be able to get what I want. I was an exemplary girl in the whole school which helped me to be ahead girl that school, got high marks in the country which helped me to attend the best school in Rwanda called Gashora Girls Academy Of Science and Technology.
